Habits

Sleep Hygiene

Build better sleep habits for improved mental wellness.

🕐 Consistent Schedule

Wake up and go to bed at the same time every day to sync your circadian rhythm.

📵 Screen Curfew

Power down all blue-light devices at least 60 minutes before sleep.

🌙 Cool Environment

Keep your bedroom temperature around 18°C (65°F) for optimal deep sleep.

☕ Caffeine Cutoff

Avoid caffeine intake at least 6-8 hours before your planned bedtime.

🛁 Wind-Down Ritual

Engage in low-stimulation activities like reading or the 4-7-8 breathing exercise.

🛏️ Bed = Sleep Only

Strengthen the mental association between your bed and rest by avoiding work or eating in bed.
