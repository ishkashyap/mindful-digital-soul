Understanding Mental Health Conditions

Learn about common mental health conditions and their symptoms.

Depression

Persistent feelings of sadness, hopelessness, and loss of interest in activities. Symptoms may include changes in sleep, appetite, and energy levels.

Mood Disorder Treatable

Anxiety Disorders

Excessive worry, fear, or nervousness that interferes with daily activities. Includes generalized anxiety, panic disorder, and social anxiety.

Common Manageable

Stress & Burnout

Physical and emotional exhaustion caused by prolonged stress. Common among students and working professionals.

Work-Related Preventable

Sleep Disorders

Conditions that affect the quality, timing, and amount of sleep. Poor sleep can significantly impact mental health.

Sleep Health Impact

Academic/Work Pressure

Excessive pressure from academic or professional demands that can lead to mental health challenges.

Pressure Performance

Financial Stress

Anxiety and worry related to financial situations, which can significantly impact overall mental well-being.

Stress Economic
